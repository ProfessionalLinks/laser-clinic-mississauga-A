Preparing for a Laser Treatment

Before undergoing any laser treatment, it is essential to follow proper pre-treatment guidelines to ensure the best results. For instance, avoid excessive sun exposure for at least two weeks prior to your appointment, as tanned or sunburned skin can increase the risk of side effects. Additionally, discontinue any exfoliating or retinol-based skincare products a few days before your session, as these can make your skin more sensitive to the laser.
